BuddhaMecha

a digital Buddha Machine

The Buddha Machine is a small plastic box that plays meditative music composed by Christiaan Virant and Zhang Jian.

This digital implementation of it lets you hear the background music, change it with the space bar and make a tibetan bell sound with a click of your mouse

BuddhaMecha, made by marianosimone for the Creative Programming for Digital Media & Mobile Apps course at Coursera
